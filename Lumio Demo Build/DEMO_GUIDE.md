# Lumio — Demo Guide

A short walkthrough for presenting this build to a prospective client.

---

## Suggested walkthrough (≈15 minutes)

### 1. Workspace overview (2 min)
- Start at **Projects**. Point out **Continue Working**, **Folders**
  (Onboarding / Compliance / Product Training), and the **All Projects** grid.
- Open **Recent** and **Trash** to show workspace organisation — these mirror
  familiar patterns from tools like Google Drive or Notion, so the workspace
  feels immediately familiar.

### 2. ID Academy (1–2 min)
- Click **ID Academy** in the sidebar. Open one or two learning paths (e.g.
  "Foundations of Instructional Design") to show the built-in guidance content
  aimed at non-specialist course authors.

### 3. Course Wizard (3 min)
- From Projects, click **+ Create New** and walk through the wizard:
  "Help Me Decide" → objectives → **Theme Designer** (palettes, fonts, button
  styles, corner radii, **page background** — now including White and Light
  Grey options) → review.
- This is the best place to show how quickly a brand-aligned course shell can
  be produced.

### 4. Course Landing Page — three themes (4 min)
Open each of the three demo courses in turn and compare:
- **New Hire Onboarding** — Indigo Focus theme, Centered hero layout, Soft
  Gradient background, pill-shaped buttons.
- **Workplace Safety Basics** — Teal Calm theme, Text Left / Image Right
  layout, Light Grey background, square buttons.
- **Formula 1 Fundamentals** — Sunset theme, Full Banner hero layout, White
  background, rounded buttons, larger display type.

This is the strongest moment to demonstrate **how differently Lumio can look
per course** while remaining the same underlying product — useful for clients
who manage multiple brands or business units.

- Open **Settings** on any course to show the Theme Engine, Hero Image
  controls, and landing page configuration live.

### 5. Lesson Builder (3–4 min)
- Open a lesson (e.g. "Personal Protective Equipment" in Workplace Safety
  Basics, or "Race Weekend Format" in Formula 1 Fundamentals).
- Show the **block library** sidebar — Text, Statements, Lists, Tables, etc.
- **Drag a new block** into the canvas and **reorder** existing blocks to
  demonstrate the editing experience.
- Highlight the **Statement blocks** (Info, Tip, Success, Warning, Error,
  Note) — each has its own icon, colour, and typography controls.
- Open the **AI Assistant** panel to show the simulated authoring assistant.

### 6. Learner Preview (2 min)
- Click **Preview as Learner** on any course.
- Switch between **Desktop / Tablet / Mobile** preview modes to show
  responsive behaviour.
- Step through a lesson, including a **Knowledge Check**, to show the learner
  experience end-to-end.

---

## Key differentiators

- **Per-course branding** — colours, fonts, button styles, corner radii, and
  page backgrounds are all configurable per course, not locked to a single
  workspace theme.
- **Flexible landing page layouts** — 5 hero/landing layouts (Centered, Text
  Left/Image Right, Full Banner, Split Screen, Minimal) to suit different
  content types.
- **Rich, structured authoring** — 12 categories of content blocks, including
  a dedicated Statements family for callouts, tips, and warnings.
- **Built-in instructional design guidance** — ID Academy and the AI
  Assistant lower the barrier for non-specialist authors.
- **True responsive preview** — Desktop/Tablet/Mobile preview modes let
  authors check the learner experience before publishing.

## Features completed so far

- Projects / Recent / Trash workspace
- ID Academy (9 learning paths)
- Course Wizard (Help Me Decide, objectives, Theme Designer)
- Theme Engine, incl. expanded Page Background options (White, Light Grey,
  Flat, Aurora Mesh, Soft Gradient)
- Hero Image system (layouts, overlays, positioning, text styling)
- Course Landing Page (5 layouts) and Landing Page Sections
- Lesson Builder with drag-and-drop, block reordering, and 12 block
  categories
- Statement block family (Info, Tip, Success, Warning, Error, Note)
- Learner Preview with Desktop / Tablet / Mobile responsive modes
- Course Settings (Theme / Hero / Landing configuration)
- Three fully-populated demo courses showcasing the above
